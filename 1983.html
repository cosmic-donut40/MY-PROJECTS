The Super Computer Prediction

According to BETSiE Super Computer, previous year’s runners-up Gujarat Titans have the highest probability to finish both as league toppers and win the IPL. Defending champions Chennai Super Kings come next in the Super Computer’s prediction as the probable champions, followed by Mumbai Indians and Lucknow Super Giants. Sunrisers Hyderabad, who are hoping to bring back their glory days under new skipper Pat Cummins, have been tipped to finish at the bottom of the standings by the Super Computer.
